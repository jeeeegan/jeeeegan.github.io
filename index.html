Why Join Us?

"The best hockey club in the world" - Shez 2024

We are the finest hockey club in the world, composed of 3 men's teams and 5 women's teams we accommodate people of all levels from pros to complete beginners!

There are so many amazing people you will meet here, team mates, friends, even future colleagues!

Traditionally a medics team, yet will welcome those from all degrees. We train on Monday for Mens 1's 2's and 3's and Womens 1's and 2's, Tuesday for Womens 3's 4's and 5's. Matches are on Wednesdays followed by a fun themed social!

What have we achieved?

  • 23/24 Mens LUSL cup winners
  • Mens 1's won tier 1 BUCS
  • Highest ranking university hockey team in London
  • Undefeated in Varsity and Macadam in over a decade
  • Club of the year 22/23
  • Sportsman of the year 23/24 : Sam De Vaux
